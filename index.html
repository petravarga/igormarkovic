My Cocktails

Re-mix Fizz

This one is one of my favorites. Refreshing and sweet, excellent for hot summer days and nights. It is made with pomegranate infused gin which is really delicious, sweet & sour mix is a must, and peach sparkling water (called Remix). If you can’t find peach sparkling water you can use regular sparkling water or some other flavor.

Bloody Jerry

Okay this probably sounds familiar… Yes it’s Bloody Mary’s boyfriend. This drink was made from ingredients I found in my fridge so you probably don’t have them, but I can tell you what it is, maybe you’re creative and can make it. So the ingredients are like in usual bloody mary cocktail, but I added ajvar (if you don’t know what it is google it, because it’s hard to explain), and I added some garlic and carrot paste (similar to ajvar), which my grandmother makes at home… Sounds weird, probably male population will like this more.

Buttered Old Fashioned

I’m a big lover of Old Fashioned, so I had to experiment a little bit with that also. Even though the real recipe is perfect to me, this one also blew my mind. The only secret, if I can call it a secret, is in the buttered bourbon. It is pretty simple to do it. Maybe sounds strange, but trust me you won’t regret trying it.

Cranberry Scarf

The only one in this collection that can be called a winter cocktail, aaaaaand it’s awesome! Really simple to make and with all the tea, you can barely know that it has any alcohol inside, but trust me, after few of these, you’ll feel it.. If you like to experiment, you can use some other flavors of tea, but don’t forget to add whiskey inside (I used whiskey infused with apples and cinnamon which gives it extra wintery taste).

Gym Beam Without Beam

You’re probably wondering about the name… Well it’s a product of my lack of inspiration. And no, there is no Jim Beam in this cocktail, but it sounded kind of cool. So this one has only 50 calories and you would never guessed it, but tastes pretty great. Green tea, no calorie simple syrup, and ofcourse vodka, for all the gym enthusiasts who look after their body and their calorie intake, this one is for you, and trust me you’ll love it.

Milky Espresso

Similar to classic Espresso Martini, but with some added flavors like Milky Choco Capuccino, which gives this cocktail a chocolate finish.

Invisible Pickles

This mixture can also be in the group of detox cocktails. And the reason for its name is because I used a juice from sour pickles. Tastes strange, but pickle juice is known to be good for releasing menstrual pain, so this one is for you ladies.
